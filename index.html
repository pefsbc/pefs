About PEFS

The Canadian Physical Activity Guidelines state that adults aged 18–64 should accumulate at least 150 minutes of moderate- to vigorous-intensity aerobic physical activity per week to start looking and feeling healthier.

PEFS is here to help you get there. Our goal is to promote fitness and wellness activities for members through education, communication, and programs. We offer a variety of classes and fitness centre options that fit into your lunch hour or right after work.

The Provincial Employees (Victoria) Fitness Society is a non-profit organization established under the Society Act in April 1997. The Society is managed by a volunteer Board of Directors.

PEFS contracts the day-to-day management of its facilities and programming to All About Fitness Ltd. This includes the fitness centres at 1515 Blanshard Street and 4000 Seymour Place, on-site fitness classes, and off-site registered programs.

For more information about the Board and governance, see Board of Directors.

For general information contact Donna Renaud, PEFS Manager at fitnesscentre@gov.bc.ca.

Facilities

PEFS currently operates two fitness facilities for eligible BC provincial employees:

4000 Seymour Place

Located in Saanich (near Blanshard and Cloverdale), this spacious, well-equipped facility supports strength and cardio training for individual workouts and small group sessions. Check the booking site for access rules and availability. Note: some drop-in options are restricted to specific employee groups — see the Membership section.

Phone: 778-698-3777

1515 Blanshard (Victoria Studio)

Our downtown Victoria studio hosts a rotating slate of instructor-led classes (noon-hour and after-work) and is configured for a range of group formats and general fitness use. To reserve a spot, use the booking site.

See the Class Descriptions and Class Schedule for programming details.

Membership & Pay Per Use

To be eligible for Membership and participate in PEFS programming, a person must be an eligible BC provincial government employee or otherwise meet PEFS eligibility criteria (as set out in the Society's bylaws and policies).

Usage Membership Options (Payroll Deduction)

Membership fees are processed via payroll deduction and provide ongoing access to PEFS facilities and classes.

Full Membership — Facilities & Classes

~$20.98 biweekly

$19.98 biweekly plus taxes. Includes access to any/all PEFS facilities and unlimited access to scheduled studio classes at 1515 Blanshard.

Equipment-Use — 4000 Seymour

~$16.78 biweekly

$15.98 biweekly plus taxes. Includes access to 4000 Seymour for equipment use only (classes not included).

Registered Drop-In / Pay Per Use

  • 1515 Blanshard (All eligible employees) — Book and pay for individual classes or packages online via Acuity. Secure payment via Stripe. No membership form required.
  • 4000 Seymour (CITZ staff only) — Pre-booked drop-in gym access available for $5 per visit.

To manage bookings or purchase drop-in passes, use the Acuity Booking site.

Class Descriptions

PEFS offers a range of in-person classes at 1515 Blanshard and other locations. Examples of classes offered (subject to seasonal changes and instructor availability):

Strength & Conditioning

  • Body Shop – A dynamic strength and conditioning class using a variety of equipment and body-weight exercises for a full-body workout.
  • TRX / Circuit – Interval-style classes combining resistance training, cardio bursts, and functional movement patterns.

Cardio & Dance

  • Bellyfit / Dance-based – Cardio classes combining simple dance-inspired moves with strength and core work.
  • Cardio Mix – Instructor-led cardio workouts designed to get your heart rate up and boost endurance.

Mind-Body & Mobility

  • Yoga / Stretch – Classes focusing on mobility, balance, and relaxation.
  • Pilates / Core – Focused core-strengthening sessions to support posture and back health.

For the latest list of active classes and seasonal offerings, refer to the current class schedule or our booking site.

Registration & Forms

Depending on how you participate (payroll-deduction membership vs. pay-per-use), you may need to complete certain forms.

For Payroll-Deduction Memberships

  • Registration / Cancellation Form — Required for setting up or cancelling your PEFS membership via payroll deduction.
  • Waiver — Must accompany all registration forms.

Forms are available through PEFS centres and internal resources. If you're unsure, email fitnesscentre@gov.bc.ca for guidance.

For Pay-Per-Use / Drop-In

Pay-per-use bookings at 1515 Blanshard and 4000 Seymour are handled directly through Acuity. No membership form required, but you may be asked to agree to a waiver online or on-site.

Visit the booking page to view options and pricing: Acuity Booking.
